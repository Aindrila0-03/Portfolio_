About Me

I am a recent graduate and enthusiastic GIS and Remote Sensing professional with a strong academic foundation in geospatial sciences. I specialize in spatial data analysis, drone-based photogrammetry, LiDAR processing, and geospatial data science, with hands-on experience using ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Google Earth Engine, Agisoft Metashape, Python, SQL, and R for spatial workflows and predictive modeling.

Currently, I am working as a Technical Executive (Drone Data Processing) at Matrix Geo Solutions Ltd, where I handle RTK/PPK aerial survey datasets, generate high-precision orthomosaics, DSM/DTM models, 3D reconstructions, and conduct volumetric analysis to support engineering and environmental projects. I collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to deliver accurate geospatial outputs for real-world industrial applications.

During my academic journey and internships, I worked on impactful projects including ConvLSTM-based agricultural drought prediction (92% accuracy), flood susceptibility modeling using Fuzzy AHP vs Random Forest, Smart Mobility solutions for metro cities, wildfire risk mapping, and 3D building & canopy height extraction using LiDAR.

My goal is to contribute to innovative geospatial projects supporting urban planning, environmental monitoring, disaster resilience, and infrastructure development. I am a quick learner, team player, and passionate problem-solver, committed to transforming spatial data into meaningful insights that drive informed decision-making.
